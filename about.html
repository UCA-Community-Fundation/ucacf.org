Empowering Our Communities

Through strategic investments and grassroots support, we aim to uplift communities, foster leadership, and build a brighter future for generations to come.

Our Mission

We provide microgrant ($500 to $5,000) of seed fund to startup charitable community projects aiming to enrich and empower Chinese American communities through civic engagement, education, heritage sharing, youth development and a greater understanding between the people of the United States and China.

Grant Eligibility

The applicant project should be sponsored by a local Chinese American community organization. The objective of the project must be well aligned with the mission of UCA and UCA Community Fund, and the project should have the potential of great and broad social impact.

Our Team

Our Board of Trustees and amazing team of volunteers are committed to serve our community. We come from different age groups, regions and professions. We take our convictions and turn them into action. Think you would be a good fit? Contact us for more information!
