重要活动 | Featured Highlight

2026第三届波士顿春晚再创辉煌，点亮麻州新年

The 3rd Boston Lunar New Year Gala Shines Again, Lighting Up Massachusetts 2026第三届波士顿春晚再创辉煌，并继续获得麻州政府与波士顿市政府的官方认可与大力支持。麻州州长 Maura Healey 与副州长 Kimberley Driscoll 共同签发嘉奖状表彰春晚对社区文化与多元发展的贡献；同时，麻州政府宣布 2026年2月15日为麻州“农历新年日”。多位州、市政要出席并致辞，肯定华裔社区对波士顿城市发展与文化多样性的长期贡献，春晚也再次成为连接不同族裔、促进理解与团结的重要时刻。

The 3rd Boston Lunar New Year Gala in 2026 once again received strong official recognition and support from the Commonwealth of Massachusetts and the City of Boston. Governor Maura Healey and Lieutenant Governor Kimberley Driscoll jointly issued a citation honoring the gala’s contribution to cultural and multicultural development, and Massachusetts officially designated February 15, 2026 as “Lunar New Year Day”. State and city leaders attended and delivered remarks, recognizing the Chinese American community’s contributions and reaffirming Lunar New Year as a unifying moment that strengthens cross-cultural understanding and community cohesion.

上月，Alaska Chinese Association 举办 2026 Chinese New Year Celebration，并同步庆祝协会 50周年 Jubilee。五十年发展历程，见证了华人在阿拉斯加的扎根、互助与传承，也让我们看到华人社区在全美各地持续发光发热。

in February, Alaska Chinese Association’s celebrated 2026 Chinese New Year Celebration, which coincided with its 50th Anniversary Jubilee. This milestone reflects decades of resilience, mutual support, and cultural continuity—showing how Chinese American communities across the country continue to thrive and contribute.

接下来重点宣传 | What’s Next: Key Promotions

3/5 Webinar：基金申请宣讲会 | March 5 Webinar: Grant Application Info Session

我们将在 3月5日 与NECCA一起举办线上宣讲会，介绍基金申请流程与2026年申请相关工作重点，欢迎更多组织与个人参与，了解如何准备更有竞争力的申请。

On March 5, UCACF will host an online webinar partner with NECCA to walk through the grant application process and share key priorities and plans for 2026. We welcome organizations and community members to join and learn how to prepare stronger applications.

America250：让社区行动起来

2026年美国建国250周年，我们鼓励各地社区组织策划活动，共同讲述华人在美国历史与社会发展中的贡献。UCACF也将持续重点支持相关项目。

As the U.S. marks its 250th anniversary, we encourage local communities to organize meaningful programs that highlight Chinese Americans’ contributions to American history and society. UCACF will continue to prioritize support for America250-related initiatives.

7月拉斯维加斯华人大会 | July in Las Vegas: UCA National Convention

今年7月，UCA将在拉斯维加斯举办全国华人大会。我们将把它作为全国社区联动与能力建设的重要平台，也欢迎更多组织加入，共同推动更有凝聚力、更有影响力的华人社区网络。

This July, UCA will host its National Convention in Las Vegas—an important platform for national collaboration, community capacity-building, and leadership connection. We welcome more organizations and partners to join us in strengthening a united, impactful Chinese American community network.

二月让我们看到：当社区持续投入、组织团结协作，我们的文化就能被看见，我们的声音就能被听见。欢迎继续关注UCACF的项目与活动，与我们一起把影响力扩展到全国更多城市与社区。

February reminds us that when communities stay engaged and organizations work together, our culture becomes visible and our voices become heard. Thank you for supporting UCACF—let’s continue expanding impact across more cities and communities nationwide.
