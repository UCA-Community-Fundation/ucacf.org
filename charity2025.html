Our History

UCACF is a non-profit organization and subsidiary of United Chinese Americans (UCA) dedicated to providing microgrants, or "angel funds," to charitable community projects and programs with the aim of enriching and empowering the Chinese American and AAPI communities through civic engagement, education, heritage sharing, youth development, and promoting a greater understanding between the people of the United States and China.

Our Programs

We award microgrants $500~$5,000 to start-up charitable community projects/programs. We accept grant applications online. Our application process is simple and easy! To apply for our microgrant, please scan the next QR Code.
